My Projects

Unicorn Bakery

Unicorn Bakery is a delightful online presence for a fictional bakery. The website features an interactive menu showcasing a variety of delicious pastries, cakes, and bread. Users can place orders, explore the bakery's story, and get in touch with the team for custom orders.

Turio Tour & Travels

Turio Tour & Travels is a comprehensive travel website designed for avid explorers. It provides information about various travel destinations, tour packages, and travel tips. Users can plan their dream vacations, book tours, and share their travel experiences through the integrated blog feature.

Silicon Mobile

Silicon Mobile is a sleek and modern website designed for a fictional mobile app development company. The website showcases the company's portfolio of mobile applications, highlights key features of their development process, and includes a contact form for potential clients. It emphasizes a clean design and user-friendly interface.
